Who We Are

Soutas Import Agencies is a trusted wholesale importer of premium batteries and energy solutions. With a legacy of reliability and excellence, we partner with top-tier suppliers to deliver high-performance products across multiple industries.

Founded in 1994, Soutas Import Agencies has built a long-standing presence in the automotive battery and lubricant import sector, proudly serving the market for over 30 years. We have earned a reputation for quality, innovation, and service excellence in the distribution of high-quality products, offering flexible solutions and personalized service to our clients.

With decades of experience in the industry, Soutas Import Agencies partners with leading global suppliers renowned for their reliability and product excellence in the automotive battery and lubricant markets. Among our esteemed battery partners are DN Automotive Corp. (South Korea), Monbat AD (Bulgaria), the Chinese powerhouse Camel Group, and Malaysian energy solutions manufacturer JUST Energy — all manufacturers of advanced batteries for cars, trucks, boats, and specialized applications.

Committed to quality, reliability, and mutually beneficial cooperation, Soutas Import Agencies continuously strives to meet customer needs and foster growth in the Greek market.

Nikolaos Soutas, founder of Soutas Import Agencies.

Our Suppliers

DN Automotive Corp.

DN Automotive Corporation is a South Korean company specializing in automotive parts, batteries, and machine tools. Founded in 1971 as Dong-Ah Tire & Rubber, it has grown into a global player with a strong presence in the automotive industry.

The company operates through several key divisions:

  • VMS (Vibration Management Systems): DN Automotive is a top supplier of anti-vibration components, including engine mounts and bushings, serving major automakers like GM and Stellantis.
  • BTS (Battery Technology Systems): Since entering the battery business in 1999, DN Automotive has developed and supplied various automotive batteries, including AGM batteries for start-stop vehicles.
  • TUBE Division: Specializes in automotive tubes, flaps, and rubber products, maintaining a strong global presence.

In 2022, DN Automotive expanded its capabilities by acquiring Doosan Machine Tools, now known as DN Solutions, enhancing its position in the machine tool industry. Headquartered in Yangsan-si, South Korea, DN Automotive continues to innovate and grow in the global automotive and machinery sectors.

MONBAT AD

Monbat AD is a leading Bulgarian battery manufacturer, founded in 1959 and headquartered in Sofia. The company specializes in producing lead-acid and lithium-ion batteries for various applications, including automotive, industrial, marine, and military uses. Monbat operates production and recycling facilities across Bulgaria, Romania, Serbia, and Italy, with a recycling capacity of 158,000 tonnes of used lead-acid batteries per year.

In 2017, Monbat expanded into lithium-ion technology by acquiring German companies Gaia Akkumulatorenwerke and EAS Germany, now operating under EAS Batteries GmbH. The company is listed on the Bulgarian Stock Exchange under the ticker MONB and employs over 1,300 people worldwide.

Camel Group Co., Ltd.

Camel Group Co., Ltd., founded in 1980, is Asia's largest car battery manufacturer, specializing in lead-acid and lithium-ion batteries. Headquartered in Xiangyang, China, the company operates eight production bases and four R&D centers, with over 7,300 employees.

Camel's product lineup includes automotive starter batteries, start-stop batteries, heavy-duty truck batteries, marine batteries, and lithium-ion solutions for electric vehicles and energy storage systems. The company also emphasizes sustainability through battery recycling and green energy initiatives.

With a global presence in over 80 countries, Camel serves both OEM and aftermarket clients, including major automakers. The company is publicly listed on the Shanghai Stock Exchange under the ticker 601311.

JUST Energy

Established in year 2016 in Malaysia, Just Energy Sdn. Bhd. is a high-tech enterprise focusing on the research, development, production and sales of automotive lead-acid batteries and accessories. It is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Zhejiang Just Electrical Appliances Co., Ltd. Its headquarters and R&D base are located in Nubu Industrial Park, Lanxi City, Zhejiang Province, China.

The parent company Zhejiang Just Electrical Appliances Co., Ltd. (formerly known as Hangzhou Battery Factory) was founded in 1979. The company holds the position of Vice Chairman for Lead-acid Battery Branch of the China Electrical Equipment Association, Executive Director for China Battery Industry Association, and Vice Chairman for Zhejiang Battery Industry Association. The company has a history of more than 40 years in the production of battery product and is renowned as a high-tech enterprise specializing in the production of high-performance batteries.
